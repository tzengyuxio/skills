## 範例一：雨天故事（中文源）

源文：[weather-rain.md](../../examples/source-texts/weather-rain.md)

> 昨天下午我在圖書館看書的時候，突然下起了大雨。雖然我沒帶傘，但還是決定走路回家。路上人很多，到處都是撐著傘的行人。我全身都淋濕了，回到家才發現鑰匙忘在辦公室了。鄰居看到我的樣子，笑著說：「你怎麼不叫計程車？」我只好苦笑著回答：「我忘了帶錢包。」

### Light

> Yesterday afternoon, I was reading at the library, then suddenly the rain came down very heavy. Although I didn't bring umbrella, I still decided to walk home lah. The road got a lot of people, everywhere all holding umbrella. My whole body wet already, only when I reached home I realised my key was at the office. My neighbour saw me like that, laughed and said, "Why you didn't call taxi?" I could only smile and say, "I forgot to bring my wallet."

### Medium（預設）

> Yesterday afternoon I at library reading book, den suddenly rain damn heavy. Although I never bring umbrella, but still decide walk home. Road got a lot of people, everywhere all got people holding umbrella. I whole body all wet already, go back home den discover key forget at office liao. Neighbour see me like dat, laugh and say lor: "Eh, how come you never take taxi?" I can only smile and say: "I forget bring wallet."

### Heavy

> Yesterday afternoon hor, I at library read book, den suddenly rain sibei heavy sia. Although I never bring umbrella, but still decide to walk home lor, what to do. Wah lau, road got damn lot people, everywhere all got people holding umbrella one. I whole body wet until like siao, go back home den realise key forget at office liao. Jialat. Neighbour see me like dat, laugh laugh say: "Oi, how come you never take cab ah?" Alamak, I can only bitter smile lor: "Paiseh, I forget bring wallet."

## 範例二：小王子（英文源）

源文：[little-prince.md](../../examples/source-texts/little-prince.md)

> The little prince lived alone on a tiny planet, no bigger than a house. He had three volcanoes to clean, and a rose that he loved very much. Every morning, he would clean his volcanoes and water his rose. One day, he decided to leave his planet to explore the universe. He was sad to say goodbye, but he knew he had to go.

### Light

> The little prince lived alone on a tiny planet, not bigger than a house. He had three volcanoes and one rose that he loved very much. Every morning he would clean his volcanoes and water his rose. One day he decided to leave his planet to go explore the universe lah. He was sad to say goodbye, but he knew he must go.

### Medium

> Little prince one person live on one small small planet, not bigger than house. He got three volcano and one rose, he damn love one. Every morning he go clean volcano and water his rose. One day he decide to leave his planet, go explore universe. He very sad to say goodbye lah, but he know must go already.

### Heavy

> Dis little prince ah, ownself live on one damn small planet, not bigger than house nia. He got three volcano and one rose, wah he sibei love one sia. Every morning he wake up den go clean clean volcano, water his rose. Den one day hor, he decide want to leave his planet, go see see de universe. Damn sad to say goodbye leh, but he know oreddy must go. What to do, like dat lor.

## 範例三：差不多先生傳（中文源 → Singlish）

源文：[mr-chabuduo.md](../../examples/source-texts/mr-chabuduo.md)（胡適 1924，公有領域）

> 你知道中國最有名的人是誰？他姓差，名不多，是各省各縣各村人氏。差不多先生的名字天天掛在大家的口頭，因為他是中國全國人的代表。他常常說：「凡事只要差不多，就好了。何必太精明呢？」他在學堂的時候，先生問他：「直隸省的西邊是哪一省？」他說是陝西。先生說：「錯了。是山西，不是陝西。」他說：「陝西同山西，不是差不多嗎？」

### Light

> Do you know who is the most famous person in China? His surname is Cha, name is Buduo. Every province, county, and village got people like him. The name "Mr. Chabuduo" is on everyone's lips every day, because he represents all the people in China. He always says, "Anything chabuduo also can lah. Why must so serious?" When he was in school, his teacher asked, "What province is to the west of Zhili?" He said Shaanxi. Teacher said, "Wrong, it's Shanxi, not Shaanxi." He said, "Shaanxi and Shanxi, chabuduo only what."

### Medium

> You know who is China most famous person? His name Cha Buduo, every province every county every village all got him one. Mr. Chabuduo name every day on everybody mouth, because he is all China people representative lor. He always say: "Anything chabuduo can already, why must so on?" When he in school time, teacher ask him: "Zhili west side is which province?" He say Shaanxi. Teacher say: "Wrong leh, is Shanxi not Shaanxi." He say: "Shaanxi and Shanxi, chabuduo only what."

### Heavy

> Eh you know de most famous person in China is who or not? His name Cha Buduo lah, every province every county every village all got him one sia. Mr. Chabuduo de name every day on everybody mouth, becos he is whole China representative mah. He always say one: "Wah anything chabuduo can already lor, why must so on?" When he go school time hor, teacher ask him: "Zhili west side is which province ah?" He say Shaanxi lor. Teacher say: "Alamak wrong leh, is Shanxi not Shaanxi." He say: "Aiya Shaanxi Shanxi, chabuduo only what, like dat lor."

## 借詞示範表

Singlish 詞彙融合多種語言，以下為核心借詞與其來源：

| 詞 | 來源 | 意思 | 例 |
|---|---|---|---|
| lah | 閩南語 | 緩和語氣 | "Cannot lah" |
| lor | 粵語 | 無奈 | "Like dat lor" |
| sibei | 福建話 | 非常 | "Sibei hot today" |
| shiok | 馬來語 | 過癮 | "The food damn shiok" |
| kena | 馬來語 | 被（被動） | "He kena scolded" |
| paiseh | 福建話 | 不好意思 | "Paiseh ah" |
| jialat | 福建話 | 糟糕 | "Wah, jialat" |
| alamak | 馬來語 | 哎呀 | "Alamak, forget liao" |
| sia | 馬來語 | 強調 | "Damn good sia" |
| makan | 馬來語 | 吃 | "Go makan" |

## 強度速查

| 強度 | 啟用範圍 | 典型特徵 | 場景 |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Light** | G1–G4 + D1–D3 | 不改拼寫、不用借詞、偶爾語氣詞 lah | acrolect — 受過教育的新加坡人輕鬆聊天 |
| **Medium**（預設） | 全套 G + D + V（核心借詞） | 基本拼寫變體（dun, dat, dis）、大多數句帶語氣詞 | mesolect — 一般日常對話 |
| **Heavy** | 全套含 S 類 | 密集語氣詞、擴展借詞、全套方言拼寫、感嘆詞（wah lau, alamak） | basilect — hawker centre 對話風格 |
